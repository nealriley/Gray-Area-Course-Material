Penguin

In this assignment you will write a program that displays a penguin or any other animal of your choice. You can use any drawing functions that you would like. Check the links below for examples of other students programs for this assignment. Your program does not have to look just like the picture of mine above, the precise picture you draw is entirely up to you -- be creative!

Don't forget to put your first name, last initial, class and assignment in comments at the top. Don't hesitate to ask for help if your aren't sure how something is suppose to work.
